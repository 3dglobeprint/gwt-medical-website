Featured Product

Saremco Print Crowntec

Crowntec is a ceramic composite resin engineered for the 3D printing of permanent dental restorations. Whether in a chairside setup or a full dental laboratory, it enables clinicians to deliver high-quality restorations in record time — from implant crowns and bridges to inlays, onlays, veneers, and full-arch denture teeth.

Beyond its outstanding physical properties ensuring longevity and durability, Crowntec excels in esthetics. Restorations polish to a high shine and can be further characterized using Saremco Els Paintart stains — specially developed for printed ceramics and compatible with zirconia work as well.

Clinical Applications

What Can You Print?

Crowntec's mechanical properties and biocompatibility make it suitable for a wide range of permanent and prosthetic dental restorations.

Permanent Crowns

Full anatomical posterior and anterior crowns with excellent marginal fit, durability, and natural esthetics matching VITA shade guide standards.

Inlays & Onlays

Chairside same-day inlay and onlay restorations. 3D printed, tried in, characterized, and cemented in a single appointment.

Veneers

Ultra-thin esthetic veneers for anterior teeth. Easy polishing and full characterization capability for lifelike results.

Bridges

Multi-unit bridge frameworks and restorations, including implant-supported cases requiring high flexural strength and long-term stability.

Full-Arch Prosthetics

Printed teeth for All-On-4 and All-On-5 full-arch restorations — both temporary and permanent — with outstanding fluorescence matching natural dentition under UV.

Denture Teeth

Individual and full-set denture teeth with natural fluorescence verified under UV lamp. Compatible with acrylic denture bases for complete prosthetic solutions.

Chairside Workflow

Same-Day Restoration in 4 Steps

From scan to cementation — all within a single appointment using Crowntec and your existing dental 3D printer.

1

Scan & Design

Intraoral scan and digital design of the restoration using standard CAD/CAM software.

2

3D Print

Print the restoration in the selected VITA shade. Crowntec is compatible with most dental 3D printers.

3

Polish & Characterize

Polish the restoration to a high shine. Optionally apply Saremco Els Paintart stains for lifelike color depth.

4

Try In & Cement

Try the restoration for fit and occlusion, then permanently cement. Patient leaves with a finished restoration.
